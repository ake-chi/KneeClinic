Knee Procedure

The knee is a complex joint and is susceptible to different orthopaedic conditions that can cause pain, discomfort, disability and disruption of daily activities. To help you fully restore your mobility, there are different procedures available for the condition affecting your knee.

Our clinic performs knee reconstruction surgery for ACL injuries and other similar cases. We also offer total knee replacements, patient-specific and partial knee replacements, and knee arthroscopy. We also recently launched computer-navigated procedures that allow us to perform robotic knee operations which are much less invasive with increased accuracy.

Read More

The most common conditions that need knee surgeries are fractures, ligament injuries, meniscus tears and knee arthritis.

Non-surgical treatment is our first option, and we only move to surgical treatments if deemed necessary both by patients and partner doctors. That is why we encourage you to have a comprehensive operative plan or discussion with our surgeons first before considering surgery. Knee surgery is the best option for certain knee problems that affect a patient’s regular activities and quality of life, and it’s important to know its potential benefits and complications.
