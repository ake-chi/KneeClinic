Rehabilitation

Promoting continuity of care

Aside from surgical and non-surgical measures, our clinic also provides advanced rehabilitation services and physiotherapy treatment for all types of knee injuries. We accept referrals from local physiotherapy and sports clinics, athlete patients who look into undergoing therapy sessions for the first time, and those who subsequently need further treatments.

During the physical therapy for a knee injury rehabilitation, the patient will have a specific set of exercises performed with our physical therapists. To strengthen and stabilize the knee joint, the exercises will include strengthening of the quadriceps, hamstrings, calf and hip.
